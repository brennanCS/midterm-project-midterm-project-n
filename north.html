Naughty or Nice


Every year Santa keeps a eye on every kid, watching their every action. Santa and his elves keep track of every good and bad action each kid takes. Santa then updates the Naughty and Nice list to decide who gets the best presents on Christmas. Those kids on the Naughty list will only recieve coal on the day of Christmas. Those kids on the Nice list will recieve a gift, specially crafted by Santa's helpers, from thier Chirstmas wishlist.
