The Christmas Tree

The Christmas Tree is a tradition that many American families participate in.

According to Britannica, originating from Germany, "families set up a paradise tree in their homes on December 24, the religious feast day of Adam and Eve."

The modern christmas tree is a universal symbol that carries both religious and secular meanings.
