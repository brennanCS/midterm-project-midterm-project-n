Santa Claus

Who is Santa Claus



- Santa Claus's full name is Saint Nicholas

- Based on a true person, Santa has given away his inherited wealth to help the poor and sick

-The image of Santa Claus in North America was influenced by Dutch immigrants



Santas Origin Story


The image of Santa Claus originates from the legend of St. Nicholas, a 4th-century Christian bishop known for his generosity/kindness

St. Nicholas was said to give away all of his inheritance to help the poor and sick
